Our Story

Welcome to Rajkot's Most Unique Dining Destination

Inspired by the nostalgic charm of railway journeys, The TrackSide Tadka offers a memorable dining experience inside a beautifully designed train-themed setting.

Whether visiting with family, friends, or colleagues, we combine delicious 100% Pure Vegetarian cuisine, warm hospitality, and a relaxing ambiance to create unforgettable moments. From authentic North Indian favorites to South Indian delicacies and Indo-Chinese specialties, every dish delivers rich flavors.
